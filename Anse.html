Anse Source d'argent


Anse Source d'argent is a beach located on the island of La Digue in the Seychelles archipelago. It is one of the most famous beaches in the world according to the Guide du routard


Geopgraphy


Two kilometers long, Anse Source d'Argent is located in the southwest of the island of La Digue . You can only access it through the Union Estate Farm, an old colonial house which houses, among other things, the giant tortoises of the Seychelles .


Geology


Some 65 million years ago, the archipelago was created by a combination of phenomena: the separation of Gondwana into an Indian tectonic plate on the one hand and an African one on the other, associated with underwater volcanism, similar to the one who created Mauritius and the island of Reunion. The granitic islands are thus in the center of the Seychelles bank, this is the case of La Digue,

Anse Source d'Argent has impressive blocks of granite arranged in a chaotic fashion on the beach. Unlike most other beaches in the Seychelles, Anse Source d'Argent is protected by a coral reef
